Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how HeKuToCbI4 ("I", "me", "my") handles information collected through the Guess the Thingy! mobile application (the "App"). I take your privacy seriously and am committed to being transparent about what data the App collects, why, and who processes it.

1. Data Collected

The App may collect the following categories of data:

  • Device information — device model, OS version, and app version.
  • Usage data — which features you interact with, screens visited, and in-app actions (if analytics is enabled).
  • Crash logs — error stack traces and app state at the time of a crash.
  • Advertising ID (AAID) — the Android advertising identifier, used for ad delivery and frequency capping.
  • Approximate location — country-level estimate, derived from IP address or device locale, used by advertising services.
  • Ad interactions — ad impressions, clicks, and whether personalized ads are enabled on your device.
  • Anonymous bug reports — when you choose to submit a bug report, the following technical details are sent along with your description: device model, Android version, SDK level, app version, build number, the screen you were on, and a timestamp. No personal identifiers are included. Submitting a report is entirely voluntary.

The App does not collect: your name, email address, phone number, precise GPS location, contacts, photos, or information from other apps on your device.

2. Why Data Is Collected

  • App functionality — device and OS info helps ensure compatibility and diagnose issues.
  • Analytics — aggregated usage data helps me understand how people use the App so I can improve it.
  • Crash reporting — crash logs let me identify and fix bugs quickly.
  • Advertising — ads displayed via Google AdMob help keep the App free. The Advertising ID and ad interaction data are used to serve and measure ads.
  • Bug reporting — anonymous bug reports you submit are stored so I can review, reproduce, and fix issues.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each of which may collect and process data according to its own privacy policy:

Google Firebase

What it does: crash reporting, analytics, and storing anonymous bug reports you choose to submit.

Data received: device model, OS version, app version, crash logs (error stack traces), usage events, occasionally country/language in aggregated form, and the contents of any bug report you voluntarily send.

Components used: Firebase Crashlytics, Firebase Analytics (if enabled), Cloud Firestore.

Google Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

What it does: serves in-app advertisements.

Data received: Advertising ID (AAID), IP address, ad impressions and clicks. If personalized ads are enabled on your device, AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show interest-based ads.

Google Privacy Policy Google Play — Advertising ID

4. Data Storage and Security

Your quiz progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. Anonymous bug reports you submit are stored in Cloud Firestore (part of Firebase). Data collected by Firebase and AdMob is processed on Google's servers and governed by Google's security practices. I do not operate my own backend or store any of your personal data on my own servers.

5. Your Choices and Control

  • Reset your Advertising ID — you can reset or delete your AAID at any time in your device's Settings → Google → Ads menu.
  • Opt out of ad personalization — disable personalized ads in your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Uninstall the App — removing the App from your device deletes all locally stored data immediately.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact me so I can assist in removing it.

7. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. For significant changes, a notice will be displayed within the App. I encourage you to review this policy periodically.
